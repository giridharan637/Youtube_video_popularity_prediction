Platform Guide

Master ViewProphet in minutes. Follow our simple 4-step workflow to unlock the power of AI-driven video analysis.

1

Enter Video Details

Head over to the Predict page. Fill in your video's title, duration, category, and potential description. You can also paste a YouTube URL to auto-fetch the thumbnail!

2

Trigger AI Analysis

Once the form is filled, click the Analyze Video Predictability button. Our advanced machine learning model will process your data against millions of historical records in seconds.

3

View Predicted Popularity

Instantly see your predicted view count and popularity level (High, Medium, or Low). Use the AI-generated SEO score and suggestions to refine your strategy before you publish.

4

Check Analytics & History

Monitor your growth in the Dashboard. Review all your past predictions in History to track your improvement as a creator.
